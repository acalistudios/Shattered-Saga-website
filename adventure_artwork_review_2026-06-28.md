## Recommended Regeneration Approach

Do not regenerate blindly from the current generic location prompts. Use story-directed prompts with:

- one recurring NPC/key figure per adventure when possible;
- consistent clothing/color descriptions for repeated NPCs;
- explicit room action, not just architecture;
- story-specific objects in the foreground;
- consistent palette per adventure;
- no readable text, no logos, no modern objects.

Recommended priority:

1. Regenerate all images marked as missing key NPC/story action in Ashveil Keep, Saltblood Mines, Iron Colosseum, Greywash Bandit Crown, Merrin Abbey, Harvest Hill, and Mirror War.
2. Lightly revise Frostfire Crypt, Blackroot Hollow, Thorn Treaty, Brass Plague, and Glass Orchard for NPC/story presence.
3. Keep most Elemental Crucible, Obsidian Vault, Sunken Spire, Astral Sky, Clockwork Conservatory, and Drowned Market as usable location plates unless stronger character continuity is desired.
